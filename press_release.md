### New Open Dataset Published: Interactive dashboard: Psychological wellbeing in researchers and university students (2019-2024). DOI: 10.7910/DVN/EZP0YO

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Interactive dashboard: Psychological wellbeing in researchers and university students (2019-2024). DOI: 10.7910/DVN/EZP0YO*.

The dataset is freely available under the **CC0 1.0 Universal** license, allowing unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction.
